we have earrings for any style: from subtle to avant-garde


no matter the size, our earrings are extremely lightweight; thanks to bone's natural porousness.

however, this also means that our earrings are more delicate than jewlery that has been made with more traditional materials.

please handle with care. all our jewlery is nickel-free.

red fox hipbone earrings

these earrings are made with real red fox hip bones, the two bones that connect with the sacrum to form the pelvis. these bones make wonderful fertility charms. the earrings have a drop length of 4 inches, and are 1 inch at the widest point.

raccoon molar stud earrings

these stud earrings are made with real raccoon molar teeth. the molars are important to the omnivorous raccoon, used to crush and grind food in the back of the mouth. each tooth is unique, but have an average circumfrence of 0.5 inches.

red fox lumbar vertebrae earrings

these earrings are made with real red fox lumbar vertebrae, and have a drop length on 1 inch. these powerful vertebrae are the largest in the body, and make up the lower back portion of the spine. these bones make wonderful strength and power charms.

squirrel jawbone earrings

these earrings are made with real squirrel jawbones. the squirrel's powerful incisors grow continuously throughout its lifetime. to accomodate them, squirrels have a hollow compartment that extends the length of the jawbone, about 2 inches.
